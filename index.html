Why Choose Razzormail?

Built for Privacy

In an age where data is the new currency, we believe your communications should remain yours alone. Razzormail was founded with a simple mission: to provide email services that respect your privacy without compromising on usability.

Unlike traditional email providers who scan your messages for advertising data, we employ zero-knowledge architecture. This means even we can't read your emails. Your data is encrypted from the moment it leaves your device until it reaches the intended recipient.

Whether you're a journalist protecting sources, a business safeguarding trade secrets, or simply someone who values personal privacy, Razzormail provides the security you need with the simplicity you want.

Our Services

Comprehensive email solutions designed for individuals and teams who refuse to compromise on security.

Personal Protection

Your personal email should be exactly that - personal. Our individual plans offer military-grade encryption, anonymous sign-up options, and no data retention. Perfect for activists, journalists, and privacy-conscious individuals who need to communicate without leaving a digital footprint.

Team Collaboration

Secure communication for modern teams. Share files, coordinate projects, and maintain client confidentiality with end-to-end encrypted group messaging. Admin controls let you manage permissions while keeping everything private. Includes shared calendars and secure file storage up to 100GB.

Enterprise Solutions

Fortune 500 companies trust Razzormail for their most sensitive communications. Our enterprise tier includes dedicated servers, custom domain hosting, compliance reporting for GDPR and HIPAA, 24/7 priority support, and advanced threat protection powered by AI-driven spam filtering.

Mobile Freedom

Your secure inbox travels with you. Our iOS and Android apps offer biometric authentication, remote wipe capabilities, and offline access to encrypted messages. Stay connected securely whether you're in a coffee shop in Berlin or a conference room in Tokyo.

Swiss Infrastructure

Your data is protected by some of the world's strongest privacy laws. Our servers are located in Switzerland, outside the reach of invasive surveillance programs. Multiple redundant data centers ensure your emails are always accessible, even during outages.

Open Source Promise

Trust through transparency. Our encryption protocols are open source and have been audited by independent security researchers. We believe you shouldn't have to trust us blindly - our code speaks for itself. Community contributions help us stay ahead of emerging threats.
